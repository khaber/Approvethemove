ATMChat — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 25, 2026

Overview

ATMChat is a decentralized offline messaging app by Approve The Move. It is designed from the ground up with privacy as a core principle.

How ATMChat Works

ATMChat sends messages directly between devices using Wi-Fi (MultipeerConnectivity) and Bluetooth Low Energy. There are no servers involved. Messages travel peer-to-peer and are never routed through the internet or any external infrastructure.

Data Storage

  • Messages — stored locally on your device only
  • Chat history — persisted on-device for your convenience
  • Device identity — a randomly generated UUID stored on your device, not linked to your Apple ID or personal information
  • Avatars and display names — stored locally and shared only with peers you connect to

Data Collection

ATMChat collects no data. Specifically:

  • No analytics or usage tracking
  • No advertising frameworks
  • No account creation — your device is your identity
  • No server-side storage or cloud sync
  • No contact list access beyond what you explicitly share
  • No location tracking

Network Activity

ATMChat has zero internet network activity. All communication happens over local Wi-Fi and Bluetooth between nearby devices. No data ever leaves your local network.

Media & Files

Photos, videos, voice messages, and files you send are transmitted directly to the recipient's device over the local connection. They are not uploaded to any server or cloud storage.

Data Deletion

You can delete individual messages (including for all participants) or clear entire conversations. Uninstalling the app removes all data permanently.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy ever changes, you will be notified within the app before any changes take effect.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact: support@approvethemove.com
