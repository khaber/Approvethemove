Why You'll Love ATM Chat

No Internet? No Problem.

We utilize an advanced hybrid networking system. If your devices are on the same local Wi-Fi network (like a hotel’s isolated VLAN), ATM Chat works instantly. No Wi-Fi? The app automatically creates a secure Bluetooth Mesh network, hopping messages from device to device.

Absolute Privacy.

You will never make an account, share an email address, or provide a phone number. ATM Chat collects zero data. You are just an anonymous peer on the local mesh. Your private conversations remain fully on-device.

Instant Setup.

Download the app, open it, and immediately discover other family members or friends nearby who also have the app open. Tap to connect and start chatting in seconds. No cloud setup required.

Rich Media Sharing.

Unlike basic mesh apps, ATM Chat is optimized for sharing. When connected to a local VLAN, seamlessly send text messages, high-resolution pictures, short videos, vCard contacts, and links.
