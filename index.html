Apps that respect you.

I got tired of seeing great app ideas locked behind paywalls and buried in ads. So I started building the apps I wanted to use — and made them free for everyone.

Hey, I'm Yousif.

I'm an iOS developer from Qatar, and I build apps under the name Approve The Move.

Here's how this started: I kept running into apps I actually wanted to use — a good finance tracker, a simple bill splitter, a solid card game — only to hit a paywall, a subscription, or a wall of ads. Every time. It felt like the most useful tools were being locked behind rent-seeking models, or worse, filled with tracking that made them painful to use.

So I started building the apps I wished existed, and giving them away. No subscriptions. No ads. No tracking. Every app under Approve The Move is completely free — and always will be.
