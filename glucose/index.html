One iPhone dashboard for every CGM. Dexcom, FreeStyle Libre, Medtronic CareLink, Nightscout, Medtrum, and Apple Health — all in one place. Built by a dad for families managing diabetes together.

What it does

GlucoRelay reads your glucose data from the cloud service you already use with your CGM provider, and shows it on iPhone, Apple Watch, Home Screen widgets, Dynamic Island, and a dedicated nightstand mode. No signup, no account creation, no advertising, no tracking. Your CGM credentials stay on your device in the iOS Keychain.

Why it exists

My son wears a Medtronic 780G pump. His cousin uses Dexcom G7. My friend's daughter just switched to a FreeStyle Libre. Every family I know juggles two or three different CGM apps that don't talk to each other.

I wanted one app where I could see all of them. Sugarmate is Dexcom-only. Happy Bob is Dexcom-only. Every vendor app supports its own brand. So I built GlucoRelay.

Supported CGM sources

  • Dexcom Share — G5, G6, G7, ONE, Stelo (real-time)
  • Dexcom API (OAuth 2.0)
  • FreeStyle LibreLinkUp — Libre 2, 2 Plus, 3, 3 Plus (11 regions)
  • Medtronic CareLink — MiniMed 670G, 770G, 780G, Guardian (40+ countries)
  • Nightscout — self-hosted
  • Medtrum EasyView — Nano, TouchCare A7+
  • Apple Health — any CGM that syncs to Health
  • CloudKit Follower — share with family using an 8-character code (no account needed)
  • Demo Data — try the app without a CGM

Features

  • Apple Watch complications for every watch face
  • Home Screen widgets in 3 sizes + Lock Screen + StandBy
  • Live Activity in Dynamic Island and Lock Screen
  • Nightstand mode — tap the glucose number for a bedside clock
  • Predictive low/high alerts with linear regression (15–30 minutes ahead)
  • Daily morning summary with overnight time-in-range
  • Universal pump status display (battery, reservoir, sensor life, IOB)
  • Critical alerts that bypass Do Not Disturb
  • Follower sharing without requiring accounts
  • CSV and PDF export with date range picker
  • Siri Shortcuts ("Hey Siri, what's my blood sugar?")

Privacy

  • Zero backend — we operate no servers
  • CGM credentials stored only in iOS Keychain
  • HealthKit access is read-only
  • No analytics, no tracking, no advertising
  • Follower sharing uses Apple CloudKit; readings expire after 24h

Important disclaimer

GlucoRelay is not a medical device. It is a viewer that reads data from the CGM cloud service you already use. Always verify readings with your primary CGM receiver before making treatment decisions. GlucoRelay is not affiliated with Dexcom, Abbott, Medtronic, Medtrum, or any CGM manufacturer.
