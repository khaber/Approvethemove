How to Use

Create your accounts — Add your bank accounts, wallets, or payment methods and set their initial balances.

Add transactions — Tap the + button on the Dashboard to record income or expenses. Assign categories and tags.

Set budgets — Go to the Budgets tab to create spending limits for any category (groceries, dining, transport, etc.).

Review reports — The Reports tab shows charts of your spending trends so you can see where your money goes.

Set up recurring entries — For regular transactions like salary or subscriptions, set them as recurring so they're added automatically.

Sync everywhere — Your data automatically syncs to all your Apple devices via iCloud. No setup needed.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is ATM Flow really 100% free?

Yes. No ads, no subscriptions, no in-app purchases, no paywalls. Download it, use every feature, keep it forever. This isn't a trial — it's the whole app.

Will my financial data be shared or uploaded anywhere?

No. All your accounts, transactions, and budgets live on your device and sync privately through your own iCloud account via CloudKit. I can't see your data — only you can.

Does it sync across my iPhone, iPad, and Mac?

Yes. ATM Flow uses CloudKit to sync across every Apple device signed into your iCloud account. Changes on your iPhone appear on your iPad within seconds.

Can I import transactions from my bank?

Yes. Export a CSV from your bank's website or app, and ATM Flow can import it. You can also set up recurring transactions for salary, subscriptions, or rent so you don't have to re-enter them.

What happens if I delete the app?

Local data on the device is removed. Your iCloud data stays safe until you manage it through your device's iCloud settings, so you can reinstall and get everything back.

Privacy

ATM Flow stores all data on your device and in your personal iCloud account. No data is sent to external servers. No analytics. No tracking.
