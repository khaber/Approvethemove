ATM Flow — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 25, 2026

Overview

ATM Flow is a personal finance tracking app built by Approve The Move. Your privacy is fundamental to how this app is built.

Data Storage

All your financial data — accounts, transactions, budgets, goals, categories, and tags — is stored in two places:

  • On your device — using SwiftData, Apple's local database framework
  • In your personal iCloud account — via CloudKit, Apple's sync service, so your data syncs across your own devices

Your data is stored in your private CloudKit container. Only you can access it through your Apple ID. The developer (Approve The Move) cannot read, access, or retrieve your data.

Data Collection

ATM Flow collects no data. Specifically:

  • No analytics or usage tracking
  • No crash reporting SDKs
  • No advertising frameworks or identifiers
  • No third-party SDKs that collect data
  • No server-side storage controlled by the developer

Network Activity

The only network activity is CloudKit sync between your own devices via your iCloud account. This is handled entirely by Apple's infrastructure and protected by your Apple ID credentials.

Photos

ATM Flow allows you to attach photos to transactions. These photos are stored locally on your device and in your iCloud. They are never uploaded to external servers.

Biometric Data

ATM Flow supports Face ID and Touch ID for app lock. Biometric data is handled entirely by iOS and never leaves the Secure Enclave on your device. The app only receives a yes/no authentication result.

Data Deletion

You can delete any data at any time within the app. Deleted data is moved to trash and can be permanently removed. Uninstalling the app removes all local data. iCloud data can be managed through your device's iCloud settings.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy ever changes, you will be notified within the app before any changes take effect.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact: support@approvethemove.com
