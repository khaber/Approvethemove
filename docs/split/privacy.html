ATM Split — Privacy Policy & Terms of Use

Effective date: April 13, 2026  |  Last updated: April 13, 2026

This document contains both the Privacy Policy and the Terms of Use for ATM Split. Please read both sections carefully before using the App.

Part I — Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how ATM Split ("the App"), developed and published by Approve The Move ("we", "us", or "our"), handles information when you ("you" or "the user") download, install, or use the App on any supported device.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy applies to all versions of ATM Split distributed through the Apple App Store and any future platforms.

By downloading, installing, or using ATM Split, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App and remove it from your device.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect, transmit, or process any personal data. ATM Split is designed from the ground up as a privacy-first application. Specifically:

  • No account creation — You are never required or asked to sign up, log in, create an account, or provide an email address, phone number, or any other personal identifier.
  • No analytics or tracking — We do not use any analytics frameworks, crash reporting services, telemetry tools, device fingerprinting, or usage tracking of any kind.
  • No advertising — The App contains no advertisements, no advertising SDKs, no ad identifiers (IDFA), and no advertising networks.
  • No cloud sync — The App does not sync data to iCloud, our servers, or any third-party cloud service. All data remains exclusively on your device.
  • No in-app purchases — No financial, billing, or payment information is collected or processed.
  • No location data — The App does not request, access, or store your geographic location.
  • No contacts or photos — The App does not access your contacts, photo library, camera, microphone, or any other device sensor beyond haptic feedback.

3. On-Device Storage

ATM Split stores data locally on your device using two Apple-provided storage systems: SwiftData (a persistent on-device database) and UserDefaults (lightweight key-value storage for preferences). All data is stored within the App's private sandbox, is not accessible to other apps, and never leaves your device.

3.1 SwiftData (Persistent Storage)

The App uses Apple's SwiftData framework to store your expense-related data on-device. This includes:

  • Expense groups (names, creation dates, currencies)
  • Members and persons within each group
  • Individual expenses (amounts, descriptions, dates, payers)
  • Split shares (how each expense is divided among members)
  • Settlements (who owes whom and how much)
  • Categories assigned to expenses

This data is stored in a local SQLite database managed by SwiftData within the App's sandbox. It is not uploaded, synced, or transmitted anywhere. There is no iCloud sync, no cloud backup of App data, and no server-side storage.

3.2 UserDefaults (Preferences)

A small amount of non-personal preference data is stored using Apple's UserDefaults system:

  • Theme preference (System, Light, or Dark)
  • Accent color selection
  • Haptic feedback preference

Both storage mechanisms are declared in our Privacy Manifest (PrivacyInfo.xcprivacy) under API category NSPrivacyAccessedAPICategoryUserDefaults with reason code CA92.1 (access to store and retrieve user preferences), as required by Apple.

How to delete: You can delete any individual group, expense, member, or settlement within the App at any time. To erase all data permanently, delete ATM Split from your device. No residual data is retained after uninstallation.

4. Network Activity

ATM Split makes one type of network request: fetching live currency exchange rates to support multi-currency expense splitting.

4.1 Exchange Rate Requests

  • Endpoint: The App retrieves exchange rate data from open.er-api.com, a public currency exchange rate API.
  • Data sent: The request contains only the base currency code (e.g., "USD", "QAR") as a URL path parameter. No personal data, device identifiers, IP-based tracking, or user information of any kind is included in the request.
  • Data received: The response contains only currency exchange rate numbers. No tracking cookies, tokens, or user-identifying data are returned.
  • Frequency: Requests are made only when needed for currency conversion and are not made on a background schedule.
  • Offline use: The App is fully functional offline for single-currency groups. Exchange rate fetching is only triggered when multi-currency support is used.

4.2 No Other Network Communication

Apart from exchange rate requests, the App does not connect to the internet, does not make any other HTTP requests, and does not transmit any data to any external server, cloud service, or remote endpoint. There is no telemetry, no analytics pings, no crash reporting uploads, and no background network activity.

5. CSV Export

ATM Split allows you to export group data as a CSV file for your own records. This export is handled as follows:

  • Generated locally: The CSV file is created entirely on your device. No data is sent to any server during generation.
  • Shared via iOS share sheet: When you export, the standard iOS share sheet is presented, giving you full control over where the file is sent (AirDrop, Files, email, etc.). We do not control, intercept, or log your sharing choice.
  • No copies retained: The App does not retain a copy of exported CSV files. Once the share sheet is dismissed, the temporary file is discarded.

6. Third-Party Services & SDKs

ATM Split does not integrate, embed, or communicate with any third-party services, SDKs, or frameworks that collect, process, or transmit user data. The App uses exclusively Apple-provided first-party system frameworks:

Framework Purpose Data Collection
SwiftUI User interface rendering None
SwiftData On-device persistent data storage None (local only)
Foundation HTTP requests for exchange rates None (no personal data sent)

We do not use Firebase, Google Analytics, Facebook SDK, Unity, AdMob, Crashlytics, Sentry, Amplitude, Mixpanel, or any other third-party data collection tool.

The exchange rate API (open.er-api.com) is a public, read-only service. We have no data-sharing agreement with this service, and no personal data is transmitted to it.

7. Apple's Data Practices

While ATM Split itself does not collect data, please be aware that Apple may independently collect certain information related to your use of the App Store, such as download statistics and crash logs. This data collection is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy, not ours, and is outside our control.

We have declared in App Store Connect that ATM Split collects no data linked to or tracking the user, as reflected on the App's App Store product page under "App Privacy."

10. Data Security

Although we do not collect or store personal data on any server, we employ the following security measures to protect your experience within the App:

  • Sandboxed storage — All on-device data (SwiftData database and UserDefaults preferences) is stored within the App's iOS sandbox, which is isolated from other apps and protected by the operating system.
  • Encrypted network requests — Exchange rate requests are made over HTTPS (TLS encryption), ensuring data in transit cannot be intercepted.
  • No persistent sensitive data — No passwords, tokens, credentials, or payment information are ever stored or processed.
  • No remote attack surface — The App does not expose any server endpoints, APIs, or network services. The only outbound request is a simple HTTPS GET for exchange rates, which transmits no personal data.

11. Data Retention

We do not operate servers, databases, or cloud storage of any kind. We do not retain any user data. All data associated with ATM Split exists exclusively on your personal device:

Data Type Storage Location Retention Period
Expense groups, members, expenses, splits, settlements, categories Device (SwiftData) Until deleted by user or App is uninstalled
User preferences (theme, accent color, haptics) Device (UserDefaults) Until App is deleted
Exported CSV files Temporary (discarded after sharing) Duration of share sheet session only

When you delete the App, all associated data is permanently removed from your device by the operating system.

12. International Considerations

ATM Split is available worldwide through the Apple App Store. Since the App does not collect or transmit any personal data to external servers, there are no international data transfers to disclose. The only outbound network request (exchange rate fetching) contains no personal data. All user-created data remains on your local device at all times, regardless of your geographic location.
