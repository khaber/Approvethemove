Qatari Joker — Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 9, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Qatari Joker ("the App"), developed by Yousif Ameen under Approve The Move ("we", "us", or "our"), handles information when you use the App. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

By using Qatari Joker, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal data. Qatari Joker is designed as a privacy-first application. Specifically:

  • No account creation — You are never asked to sign up, log in, or provide an email address.
  • No analytics or tracking — We do not use any analytics frameworks, crash reporting tools, or usage tracking services.
  • No advertising — The App contains no ads and no advertising SDKs.
  • No server-side communication — The App does not connect to the internet and does not transmit data to any external server.
  • No in-app purchases — No financial or payment information is collected.

3. On-Device Storage

Qatari Joker stores a small amount of data locally on your device to remember your preferences. This data is stored using Apple's UserDefaults system and never leaves your device. It includes:

  • Display name (player name you choose)
  • Theme preference (System, Light, or Dark)
  • Accent color selection
  • Game settings (AI difficulty, AI speed, sound effects, haptic feedback, card play confirmation)

All game state — your cards, bids, tricks, and scores — exists only in memory while you play. When you close the App, this data is discarded. There is no cloud sync, no saved game history, and no persistent game data.

You can reset all stored preferences at any time by deleting the App from your device.

4. Local Multiplayer

Qatari Joker offers local multiplayer using Apple's MultipeerConnectivity framework. This connects nearby devices directly over Wi-Fi and Bluetooth for peer-to-peer gameplay.

  • No internet connection is used. All communication happens directly between devices on the local network.
  • Data transmitted is limited to game state only: card plays, bids, trump selection, trick results, and scores.
  • Player display names are shared between connected devices during a session so players can identify each other.
  • Encryption: All peer-to-peer communication is encrypted using Apple's built-in encryption (MCSession with required encryption preference).
  • When the session ends, no multiplayer data is retained on any device.

5. Third-Party Services

Qatari Joker does not integrate any third-party services, SDKs, or frameworks that collect data. The App uses only Apple-provided system frameworks:

  • SwiftUI — User interface
  • MultipeerConnectivity — Local multiplayer networking
  • AudioToolbox — System sound effects
  • UIKit (Haptics) — Haptic feedback

None of these frameworks collect or transmit personal data to us or to any third party.

6. Children's Privacy

Qatari Joker is rated 4+ and is suitable for all ages. The App does not contain violence, gambling (real or simulated), mature content, or objectionable material.

Because we do not collect any personal information from any user, we are compliant with:

  • COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) — No personal data is collected from children under 13.
  • Apple's App Store Guidelines for Kids — No data collection, no third-party analytics, no advertising.

Parents and guardians can be assured that their children can use Qatari Joker without any privacy concerns.

7. Your Rights

We respect the privacy rights granted by applicable laws, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) for users in the European Economic Area and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) for California residents.

Under these regulations, you have the right to:

  • Access — Request a copy of any personal data we hold about you.
  • Deletion — Request that we delete your personal data.
  • Correction — Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Portability — Request your data in a portable format.
  • Opt-out — Opt out of the sale of personal information.

Since we do not collect, store, or process any personal data, there is no data for us to provide, correct, delete, or transfer. All preferences stored locally on your device can be erased by deleting the App.

We do not sell personal information to any party, as we do not collect any.

8. Data Security

Although we do not collect personal data, we take the following measures to protect your experience:

  • Local multiplayer sessions use Apple's required encryption to protect data transmitted between devices.
  • On-device preferences are stored in the App's private sandbox, accessible only to the App itself.
  • No data is transmitted over the internet at any point.

9. Data Retention

We do not retain any data on our servers because we do not operate any servers. All data associated with Qatari Joker exists exclusively on your device:

  • Preferences — Stored until you delete the App or reset settings.
  • Game state — Exists in memory only during gameplay; discarded when the App is closed.
  • Multiplayer session data — Exists only during an active session; discarded when the session ends.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the App or applicable laws. If we make material changes, we will:

  • Update the "Effective date" at the top of this page.
  • Notify you within the App before any changes take effect.

We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
