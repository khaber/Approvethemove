How to Use

Add your currencies — Tap + to add the currencies you use most to your list.

Enter an amount — Use the keypad to type a number. All conversions update instantly.

View rates — See the exchange rate used for each conversion and when it was last updated.

Refresh — Tap refresh to pull the latest rates. The app also caches rates for offline use.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where do the exchange rates come from?

Live rates are fetched from open.er-api.com, a public currency data API. No personal data is sent with the request — just the currency query.

Does it work offline?

Yes. The app caches rates so you can keep using it without internet. Cached rates are saved on your device for offline use.

How often are rates updated?

Rates refresh automatically when you open the app with an internet connection. You can also manually refresh them anytime with one tap.

How many currencies are supported?

Over 100 world currencies. You can add the ones you use most to your quick list and reorder them however you want.

Is there really no subscription or ads?

Correct. It's fully free with zero ads, zero tracking, and no "premium" version locked behind a paywall.

Privacy

Currency Converter only fetches exchange rate data from a public API. No personal data is collected or stored externally. Cached rates are stored on your device.
