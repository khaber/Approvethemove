Privacy

Last updated: March 25, 2026

The Short Version

I don't collect your data. Period. All Approve The Move apps store data on your device or in your own iCloud account. Nothing is sent to me, to third-party analytics, or to ad networks.

What This Means

  • No analytics or tracking SDKs
  • No advertising frameworks
  • No server-side data collection
  • No account creation required
  • Your data stays on your device or your iCloud
  • If anything ever changes, you'll be notified in the app
